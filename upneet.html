Uttar Pradesh Pre Medical Exam (UPNEET)
UP NEET UG Online Counseling | Registration | Choice Filling 2023
UP NEET UG 2023 Short Details of Notification
Important Dates
  • Application Begin : 25/07/2023
  • Last Date for Registration : 28/07/2023 upto 11 AM Only.
  • Registration Fee Last Date : 28/07/2023
  • Merit List Released : 29/07/2023
  • Choice Filling :31/07/2023 to 03/08/2023
  • Seat Allotment Result : 03-04 August 2023
 Application Fee
  • Registration Fees :
  • General / OBC : 2000/-
  • SC / ST / PH : 2000/-
  • Security Fees :
  • Govt Medical/Dental Colleges/Institutes/State Universities : 30,000/-
  • Private Medical Colleges/Institutes/State Universities : 2,00,000/-
  • Private. Dental Colleges/Institutes/State Universities : 1,00,000/-
  • Pay the Examination Fee Through Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking Fee Mode Only.
NEET UG 2023 Eligibility
  • Qualified in NTA NEET 2023 Exam
  • High School and Intermediate Exam Passed in Uttar Pradesh
  • Domicile Certificate in UP.
  • More Eligibility Details Read the Notification.
 NEET UG Age Limit
  • Minimum Age : 17 Years.
  • Candidate Born On or Before : 31/12/2006
How to Fill UP NEET Online Counselling Registration 2023
  • OBC & EWS Certificate Must be Issued After : 01/04/2023
  • Online counseling of Uttar Pradesh NEET UG Admission 2023 will start from 25/07/2023.
  • Those who want to get admission in the medical college of Uttar Pradesh Domicile Candidates and have passed NEET examination conducted by NTA, they can register Uttar Pradesh NEET in online counseling.
  • For More Details Must Read the UP NEET UG Counseling Notification.
